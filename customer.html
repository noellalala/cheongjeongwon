Consumer Dispute Resolution Standards

Daesang Corporation compensates according to the Consumer Dispute Resolution
Standards specified by the Fair Trade Commission.

Soft drinks, snacks, frozen desserts, dairy products, canned goods, bakery products, sugar, flour, edible oils, processed meat products, seasonings,
sauces, teas, noodles, health foods, alcoholic beverages, boxed meals, side dishes, frozen foods, drinking water.

-Spoilage, Deterioration
-Exceeded Shelf Life
-Foreign Matter Contamination		 Product Exchange or Refund of Purchase Price -
-Side Effects
