Slogan

Expressed in familiar consumer language, encapsulates the meaning of allowing for a more pleasant daily life through enjoyable meals - 'The Better Tasting Life'. Cheongwon aspires to be

'The Better Tasting Life' brand that fills life with taste across the spectrum of culinary habits, enriching life with taste. The spirit of embodying greater value today than yesterday is Cheongwon's commitment to embracing new challenges alongside its customers.

Symbol

Why are they use that words?

Cheongjeongwon’s symbol expands the 'C' of Cheongwon to fill the 'taste' felt by customers, as well as 'taste' in life, represented by a perfect circle. Additionally, the hidden leaves within the symbol symbolize Cheongwon's dedication to 'cleanliness' and 'sincerity' in achieving healthy flavors, symbolized by the Blue color.

The genuine nature of Cheongwon, permeating customers' lives with emotion, is encapsulated in the Gradation Yellow color. Cheongwon primarily uses the Blue Color BI. However, in the aspect of carrying forward the fermentation expertise of its condiment products over a long time, it applies the Red Color BI in conjunction with product line brands such as Sunchang and Sun-filled.
